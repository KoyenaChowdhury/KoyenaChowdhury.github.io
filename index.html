About

01

I am a Ph.D. Senior Research Fellow in Computer Science and Engineering at the CNerRG Lab, IIT Kharagpur, supervised by Prof. Saptarshi Ghosh. My doctoral research focuses on the application of AI in analyzing and optimizing Indian Railways — one of the world's largest and most complex transportation networks. I develop Graph Neural Networks and spatio-temporal deep learning models that capture the intricate dependency structures of railway networks to predict train delays, optimize platform allocation, and model network growth over decades.

Before IIT Kharagpur, I completed my M.Tech. at NIT Durgapur with a 9.28 CGPA (2nd Rank), where I worked on influence maximization and influential node detection in social networks and have recieved the Best Project Award in CSE department. I hold a B.Tech. from Academy of Technology with a 9.50 CGPA (2nd Rank).

Beyond research, I am passionate about teaching AI to practitioners — from government officials to ISS probationers — and have delivered lectures on AI, GNNs, and data science at various platforms. I have mentored several B.Tech., M.Tech., and dual-degree students in their research projects and have been a teaching assistant for various courses.
